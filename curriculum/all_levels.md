# Level 1 (Ages 8–10)

## Discovering How the World Works

### Goal: introduce the basic structures of reality.

Children learn to see patterns, causes, and uncertainty.

⸻

## Book 1

### Seeing Patterns

Children discover that the world contains hidden order.

Topics:
• patterns everywhere
• classification
• sequences
• analogies
• patterns in nature
• patterns in behavior
• false patterns
• superstition
• pattern prediction

Core lesson:

The world contains patterns, but not every pattern is real.

⸻

## Book 2

### Cause and Effect

Now children ask:

Why do things happen?

Topics:
• cause vs coincidence
• mechanisms
• simple experiments
• interventions
• chain reactions
• multiple causes
• testing ideas

Core lesson:

Understanding something means understanding what produces it.

⸻

## Book 3

### Chance and Uncertainty

Children learn that the world is not perfectly predictable.

Topics:
• randomness
• probability
• coin flips and dice
• prediction
• good guesses vs bad guesses
• luck vs skill

Core lesson:

Smart thinkers make better bets, not perfect predictions.

⸻

# Level 2 (Ages 11–13)

## Understanding Systems and Reasoning

### Goal: move from isolated events to systems of interacting causes.

Children begin reasoning about complexity.

⸻

## Book 4

### Models and Explanations

Topics:
• maps vs territory
• assumptions
• simplification
• prediction
• limits of models
• competing explanations

Core lesson:

Every explanation is a model of reality.

⸻

## Book 5

### Systems and Feedback

Topics:
• feedback loops
• chain reactions
• equilibrium
• complex systems
• unintended consequences

Examples:
• ecosystems
• traffic
• social systems

Core lesson:

Systems behave differently from individual events.

⸻

## Book 6

### Human Behavior

Topics:
• incentives
• cooperation
• competition
• social norms
• decision psychology

Core lesson:

People respond to incentives and environments.

⸻

# Level 3 (Ages 14–16)

## Applying Thinking to Real Decisions

### Goal: teach students to use these ideas to navigate real life.

⸻

## Book 7

### Decisions and Tradeoffs

Topics:
• opportunity cost
• decision frameworks
• risk vs reward
• long-term thinking

Core lesson:

Every decision involves tradeoffs.

⸻

## Book 8

### Truth and Evidence

Topics:
• evaluating claims
• scientific reasoning
• evidence vs opinion
• prediction vs explanation

Core lesson:

Good thinking depends on good evidence.

⸻

## Book 9

### Understanding the World

Capstone book.

Topics:
• predicting outcomes
• analyzing systems
• evaluating policies
• solving real problems

Core lesson:

The world becomes understandable when you see its structure.
