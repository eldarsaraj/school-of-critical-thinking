About the scoring

The SHSAT has 57 ELA questions and 57 Math questions, but only 47 of each are scored. The rest are unscored field-test items — you won't know which ones. Scores range from 200 to 800 (composite 400–1600, then re-scaled to 200–800).

Our platform uses a conservative scaling table. Scores shown are estimates. Actual SHSAT scaling varies by year and is not published by the NYC DOE.
