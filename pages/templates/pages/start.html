Start

The School of Critical Thinking is a small practice-driven organization focused on helping you think clearly and access the right resources to do so. We rebuild thinking from first principles for a world where answers are cheap and judgment is rare.

For adults

Tools can summarize, generate, and calculate faster than any person. The scarce skill now is judgment: reasoning under uncertainty, choosing good problems, and making decisions when there is no obvious answer.

The School works with adults who want to strengthen those foundations, not to compete with machines, but to think where machines cannot.

Contact the School

For parents

Your child is entering a world where information and answers are abundant. Advantage will come from how one thinks: framing problems, weighing evidence, and handling uncertainty.

The School works with children and adolescents to develop those skills early, so they grow into thinkers who are not dependent on tools, trends, or systems they do not understand.
